# Asthmatic Thieves

Game made for GGJ 2026 by dutudev & ale

Rob a house as a thief who suffers from asthma, try to steal as much stuff as you can, before getting exposed by cameras or losing your breath
Controls :

A & D - move

W & S - switch between items

E - pickup items

Q - drop items / use inhaler

SPACE - toggle mask
